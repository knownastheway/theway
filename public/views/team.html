TEAM

Aaron

Visionary Servant

Aaron is the lead visionary behind The Way and lives his life with his family on mission, each day where God has placed them. With a background in starting multiple businesses, as well as 10 years of discipling men, women and children, he was called to come use those gifts and talents in the lands of SE Asia approximately 7 years ago after living a similar missional life in the United States. He is a member of the Acts 29 network, and also serves alongside and partners with several mission organizations, churches, and businesses around the world. His hopes and dreams for The Way would be that it could continue to glorify God, and make healthy gospel centered disciples long after his lifetime, throughout all the nations of the world.
