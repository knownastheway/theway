Mission

We bring glory to God by being the family of God on mission to serve our world by displaying His love and Glory.

  • Reaching The Unreached
  • Prevention of Human Trafficking
  • Community Transformation
  • Creating Sustainable Opportunities

Training Indigenous House Church Leaders

We are here helping train leaders on how to make their churches healthy through gospel focused equipping. We also teach them how to reach buddhist in appropraite contextualized forms.

Bible and Gospel Material Printing / Distribution

Partnering with other local leaders to help print and distribute over 150,000 plus materials to unreached areas throughout the nation.

Church Planting

At The Way we desire to be a church that plants churches, that plants more churches in the unreached and unengaged areas of SE Asia.

Emergency Relief Work

Every couple of months there are many causes that create refugees and IDP's without access to food, shelter or clean water. We get the great privilege of working alongside local leaders to help bring in aid to these people in difficult situations.

Living Water Projects

Partnering with organizations and communities to help bring in clean drinking water to areas struck by disaster and sectarian violence.

Community and Child Development

We work in 2 main areas now alongside other NGO's and ministries to help in community transformation and child development. We believe that the gospel not only transforms the hearts of individuals, but also communities as a whole.

Traditional Pastoral Equipping

The majority of traditional pastors are steeped in poor theological training and often desire to build man's(their own) kingdom,not God's. We try to unbind this stronghold by loving and training them in Christ-centered approches to ministry.

Sacred Natural Farm

To create an environment for transforming lives holistically through the power of the gospel, by having a life on life discipleship environment.
